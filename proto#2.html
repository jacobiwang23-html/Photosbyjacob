About

Hey everyone! I'm Jacob Wang, born and raised in Queens, New York! Ever since I was a kid, I've loved sharing special moments with the people around me. Growing up with technology made it easy, I was always snapping pictures, whether it was a tasty meal or the city skyline. Capturing memories has always been my thing.

As I got older, sports opened up a whole new world for me. Beyond the games, I've seen how sports bring people together, meeting new friends, building connections, and learning lessons that stick with you for life. Take a scroll through my photos and you'll get a glimpse of how I see the world, full of energy, joy, and moments worth remembering.

Located in New York City, feel free to reach out by clicking the Email or IG links. I'm always open to new opportunities and projects. Scroll to take a look at my portfolio!
